Cinnamon Rolls Recipe:

Ingredients

Rolls

  • 3 1/4 ounces granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspooon salt
  • 3 ounces soft butter
  • 1 egg
  • Zest of lemon
  • 16 ounces bread flour
  • 1 packet instant yeast
  • 8 ounces buttermilk
  • /2 cup white sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar

Frosting

  • 2 cups powdered sugar, sifted
  • 8 ounces cream cheese at room temperature
  • 4 ounces butter at room temperature
  • Add heavy cream if needed to thin frosting

Method

  1. Combine 3 1/4 ounces granulated sugar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 3 ounces soft butter. Mix slowly at first and then speed up. Mix for about 1 minute or until fluffy
  2. Scrape down sides of bowl and add 1 whole egg and lemon zest. Mix for about 2 minutes
  3. Add 16 ounces of bread flour with packet of instant yeast and 8 ounces of buttermilk and mix with paddle lightly until just comined
  4. Place dough onto work surface and knead it for about 10 minutes to develop gluten
  5. Put dough into oiled bowl big enough to allow it to double in size and cover with plastic wrap and let sit for 2 hours
  6. After 2 hours, oil work surface and rolling pin and turn out the dough
  7. Knead dough for an additional minute to get air out and roll out to 14"x18"
  8. In a bowl combine 1/2 cup of white sugar, 1 1/2 tablespoons cinnamon, and 1/4 cup brown sugar. Mix to combine and sprinkle all over rolled out dough and then roll it up
  9. Place seam side down and trim off 1 inch from each end before sliceing into 12 equal pieces
  10. Oil and add parchment paper to large baking dish and then add cinnamon rolls. Make sure to give each roll 1/2" of space between one another
  11. Cover and proof for 90 minutes
  12. Remove plastic and brush tops with melted butter and place in oven at 375F for about 20 minutes or until brown
  13. Take rolls out of the oven and frost them almost immediately letting them cool for no more than 5 minutes
  14. Remove rolls as a whole from baking dish and place on wire rack and pull apart to serve
