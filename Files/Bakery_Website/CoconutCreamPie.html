Coconut Cream Pie Recipe:

Ingredients

Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs, ~170 grams
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar firmly packed
  • 1/2 cup melted butter

Filling:

  • 1 1/2 cups sweetened shredded coconut
  • 3/4 cup sugar, ~150 grams
  • 4 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 5 egg yolks
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream, 236mL
  • 1 cup whole milk, 236mL
  • 3 tablespoons softned butter cut into three parts
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F
  2. Combine graham cracker crumbs and brown sugar in large bowl and stir to combine
  3. Mix in the melted butter and stir until the crumbs are moistened
  4. Pour the mixture into a pie plate and firmly pack it onto the bottom and sides evenly
  5. Place it in the oven and bake for 12 minutes and allow to cool completely
  6. Reduce the oven temperature to 325F
  7. Spread coconut onto a cookie sheet and cook for 5 minutes, flip them around and cook for an additional 5 minutes until golden brown and allow to cool
  8. In a saucepan whisk together sugar, cornstarch, and salt
  9. In the same saucepan add eggs, coconut milk, heavy cream, and whole milk and whisk well
  10. Place the saucepan on stove with heat set to medium and whisk frequently till mixture thickens and starts to bubble
  11. Once bubbling whisk for 45 seconds and it should appear pudding like
  12. Remove from heat and mix in 1 piece of butter at a time until combined
  13. Add vanilla extract and stir 1 cup of the toasted coconut
  14. Pour into pie crust and wrap in plastic wrap directly against surface to prevent skin from forming
  15. Allow it to cool at room temperature and then place in fridge for 2-3 hours or overnight
  16. Top with whip cream and more coconut before serving
