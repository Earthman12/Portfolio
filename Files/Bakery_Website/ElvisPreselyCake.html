Elvis Presley Cake Recipe:

Ingredients

Cake

  • Box of yellow cake mix
  • 3 ounce box vanilla pudding not sugar free
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 1 cup milk
  • 16 ounce can crushed pineapple undrained
  • 1 cup sugar

Frosting

  • 8 ounce package cream cheese at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup butter at room temperature
  • 3 cups confectioners sugar
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract(optional)

Method

  1. Place cake mix, pudding, eggs, oil, and milk in large mixing bowl and beat for a minute. Scrape sides and beat again for another two minutes until smooth
  2. Pour batter into greased 9x13 baking dish and bake at 350F for 30-35 minutes or until golden. Will be ready when a tooth pick can be placed in the center and comes out clean
  3. While cake is baking, place pineapple and sugar into medium saucepan on stovetop and bring to a boil over medium high heat while constantly stirring until sugar dissolves and remove from heat
  4. Once cake is done, remove from oven and poke lots of holes on the surface and pour the hot pineapple mix over the top and spread and allow to cool
  5. For frosting place butter and cream cheese in large mixing bowl and beat until smooth and creamy then add confectioners sugar gradually scraping down sides as needed and stir in pecans
  6. Drop dollops of frosting onto cooled cake and slowly spread them together to cover cake. Careful not to disturb pineapple topping and sprinkle more pecans if needed
